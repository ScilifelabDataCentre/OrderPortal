  • The first item is the identifier of the column. It is required, and will be shown as a label.
  • Items on a line are separated by semi-colon ';'.
  • The second item is the type of value, currently either 'string' or 'select'. This item is optional; the default is 'string'.
  • If the type is 'select', the third item specifies the options, one after the other separated by vertical bar '|'.
