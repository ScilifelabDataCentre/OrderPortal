{# To be included in field_create.html and field_edit.html #}
  • The first item is the identifier of the column. It is required. The identifier will displayed in the header of the table.
  • Items on a line are separated by semi-colon ';'.
  • The second item is the type of value; currently 'string', 'int', 'float' or 'select'. This item is optional; the default is 'string'.
  • If the type is 'select', the third item specifies the options, one after the other separated by vertical bar '|'. An option '[no value]' may be defined; it will give an empty field.
